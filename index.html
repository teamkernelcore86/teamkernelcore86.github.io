About

Team Kernel Core 86 is a group of Lua scripters. It was originally founded to create and maintain the Noto Executor in C#, but I later decided to change it to lua scripting. Team Kernel Core 86 creates graphical user interfaces, scripts and enviroment changers to overall change the enviroment of a experience within Roblox. We mostly dedicate our time to maintain MC6 and MC7. You might be wondering what MC6 and MC7 is? Well they are graphical user interfaces used to exploit within a Roblox experience. They can change the sky, add sound, add particles and so much more. I am to lazy to write more stuff. :D

Contributing
